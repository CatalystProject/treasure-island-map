Stakeholder Democracy Network

Stakeholder Democracy Network (SDN) works to empower those that have been worst affected by the activities of extractive industries, to help them to get a better deal.

Environmental Rights Action/Friends of the Earth

Environmental Rights Action/Friends of the Earth, Nigeria is a Nigerian advocacy group dedicated to the defence of the Human ecosystem in terms of human rights. ERA/FoEN is the Nigerian chapter of the Friends of the Earth International (FoEI). ERA is also the coordinating NGO for the Oilwatch International as well as the host of the secretariat of the Africa Tobacco Control Regional Initiative (ATCRI). ERA is also the coordinating NGO for the Nigerian Tobacco Control Alliance (NTCA). Read more in their field reports.

Niger Delta Watch

Niger Delta Watch aims to use existing networks of NGOs, Civil Society Groups and communities/individuals themselves to submit reports. It is accessible to anyone with a mobile phone making it possible for remote communities to inform us of what is happening in their area by sending text messages.

OilSpillWitness

OilSpillWitness is a Project of the FOSTER/DFID to monitor and report on the Oil Spills in the Niger Delta. The Project is jointly carried out by WANGONeT, SDN, and NACGOND. The Project also integrates with the UNEP Report carried out in OgoniLand.
